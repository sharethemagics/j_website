Jarecki Valves is an American valve manufacturer specializing in high performance metal seated ball valves, control valves, and check valves. Carrying over our expertise in engineering and manufacturing severe service metal seated ball valves for the Navy Nuclear Industry, Jarecki Valves has extensive knowledge working with exotic materials.

With over forty years of providing our customers with high quality metal seated ball valves, Jarecki Valves has gained a reputation as a leader in cutting edge ball valve technology. Offering our customers improved ball designs no other competitor can offer, we provide the tightest shut-off for the longest period of time in rough applications, while improving seat life, offering lower torques, and providing a high cycle metal seated ball valve.

Jarecki Valves is proud to be ISO 9001:2008 certified. Our standard leakage class adhering to our quality of production is to make sure every valve that ships is at least ANSI Class V. We also offer MSS-SP-61, ANSI Class VI and bubble tight upon request. Jarecki metal seat ball valves are offered not only through our standard product line, but also through a custom designed valve to fit your needs in harsh environments. Proven in the field, our valves are designed to withstand corrosion, pressure surges, and abrasion better than any other valve on the market.

Innovation & Design

Application specific valve designs are born from investigative analysis. Jarecki Valves typically use a floating ball design. This allows the ball to move with the flow media against the downstream seat. The result is a bi-directional valve with effective sealing at high as well as low pressures.

Quality First

Every valve that leaves the plant has both a hydrostatic and seat leakage test performed on it. Also, any actuation which is mounted on the valve is tested at our facility as well. The customer is assured a good valve when it is put into service.
